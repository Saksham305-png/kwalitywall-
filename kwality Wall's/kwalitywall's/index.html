OUR PURPOSE

THE KWALITY WALL'S STORY: HOW IT ALL BEGAN

Kwality Wall’s is a popular Indian ice cream brand that has been delighting people for decades. It all began in 1956 when the company was founded in Delhi, India, with the vision of providing high-quality, affordable ice cream to Indian households. The brand started with a small production setup, focusing on traditional flavors like vanilla, chocolate, and strawberry, made with rich dairy ingredients.

Over the years, Kwality Wall’s expanded across India, introducing a wide variety of flavors, frozen desserts, and innovative products to cater to changing consumer tastes. They became known for combining global expertise (as part of the Unilever family) with local flavors, making their ice creams a household favorite. Today, Kwality Wall’s is synonymous with fun, indulgence, and joy, especially among children and families, offering products for every occasion—from daily treats to festive celebrations.
